Air Fryer Pizza

Experience the ultimate culinary delight with our simple and irresistible air fryer pizza, designed to please the entire family. This mouthwatering creation starts with refrigerated pizza dough, skillfully divided in half and partially baked to perfection using the innovative air fryer technique. Once the foundation is set, lavish it with generous amounts of Sargento® Shredded Mozzarella and an enticing medley of your favorite toppings. From classic pepperoni to vibrant veggies, the choice is yours. Witness the captivating transformation as the pizza undergoes the final stage of air frying, turning into a tantalizing masterpiece with a beautifully golden-brown crust.

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg. (13.8 oz.) Classic pizza dough, cut in half
  • 1 tsp. Olive oil
  • 1/4 cup Pizza sauce
  • 2 cups Sargento® Shredded Mozzarella, divided
  • 1/4 cup Italian sausage crumbles, cooked
  • 6 slices Pepperoni, quartered
  • 3 ea. Small button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup Diced onion
  • 1/4 cup Diced green bell pepper

Directions

  1. Cut 2 pieces of parchment paper to fit the inside of your air fryer. Stretch each half of pizza dough over the pieces of parchment. Brush both lightly with olive oil.
  2. Lower one dough into the air fryer basket. Turn air fryer to 400 degrees and cook for 3 minutes or until lightly browned.
  3. Flip dough with a pair of tongs, brush with oil and air fry an additional 2 minutes. Remove pizza crust and parchment paper and let cool. Repeat par-baking crust with remaining pizza dough. Let cool.
  4. Spoon 2 tablespoons of sauce onto each par-baked crust and spread with the back of the spoon.
  5. Sprinkle 3/4 cup of cheese over both pizzas.
  6. Top each with remaining ingredients. Finish each with remaining ¼ cup of cheese. Carefully place one topped pizza in the air fryer basket by using the parchment paper to lower it into the basket. Cook for 4-5 minutes at 330 degrees or until top is golden brown and ingredients are cooked through. Remove pizza with tongs or a spatula and serve.
